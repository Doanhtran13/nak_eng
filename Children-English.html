Children's English

Basic course 1

The basic course 1 helps young children gain exposure to English, increasing their love for this foreign language

Basic course 2

The basic course 2 will help children speak simple English sentences and basic grammatical structures.

Basic course 3

The basic course 3 will help children be able to communicate more advanced English structures, and be able to speak or write basic English paragraphs.

Advanced course 1

Advanced course 1 will help children become familiar with more difficult vocabulary and more advanced structures.

Advanced course 2

Advanced course 2 focuses on helping children communicate smoothly and pronounce correctly.

Advanced course 3

Advanced course 3 will help children read and understand difficult passages and write good essays using more advanced vocabulary.
